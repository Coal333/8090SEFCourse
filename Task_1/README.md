# 8090SEFCourse
Task 1

In this project, a HKMU bank account management system will be developed. It does bank operations from the
bank admins' perspective. It will do the following tasks. The tasks may be elaborated upon later on.

Customer Accounts
- Read Customer Accounts and show the Account data (Must include balance)
- Edit Customer Account information
- Transfer money from Customer Account to other Customer/Business Accounts
- Manage two accounts for each customer (Current and Savings)
- Create new Accounts
- Delete Accounts
- Merge Accounts

Business Accounts
- Do the same as the Customer Accounts
- Buy Assets (Gold & Commodities) from the Bank (Managed by the Admin Accounts)
- Store and read contracts

Admin Accounts
- Manage Assets
- Edit performance data (Of the admins)
